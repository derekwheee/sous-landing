# Privacy Policy for Sous

_Last updated: November, 2025_

Thank you for using **Sous** (“the App”). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

By using Sous, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
Sous may collect the following information when you interact with the app:
- **Account information** (such as email, name, or profile photo) if you choose to create an account or log in.
- **Recipe data**, favorites, pantry items, meal plans, or other content you add to the app.
- **Feedback or support messages** you send us.

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
When you use the app, we may automatically collect:
- **Device information** (model, OS version, app version).
- **Usage data** (screens visited, actions taken) to improve app performance and user experience.
- **Crash logs or diagnostics** to help fix bugs.

We **do not** collect precise location data.

### 1.3 Third-Party Services
Sous may use third-party services for:
- **Authentication** (e.g., Apple, Google, or Clerk)
- **Analytics** (e.g., Apple Analytics, Firebase)
- **Cloud storage** or syncing

These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide the core functionality of the app  
- Sync your data across devices  
- Improve app performance and stability  
- Personalize your experience  
- Respond to support requests  
- Monitor usage trends to improve the app over time  

We **do not sell your personal information**.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data.  
However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your recipes, pantry items, and other personal app data may be stored:
- Locally on your device  
- Securely in cloud storage if account syncing is enabled
